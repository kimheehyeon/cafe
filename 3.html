Ediya

Ediya Coffee (이디야 커피) is a coffeehouse chain based in South Korea. As of 2019, the chain has 3,000 retail stores in South Korea. Since opening its first store in 2001, it has opened its 100th store in August 2003, the 200th store in October 2006, and the 300th shop in March 2009. As of September 2016, it had more than 2,000 stores after opening its 2000th location in Yongin, near Seoul.[3] In September 2005, it opened a branch in Beijing, China. In February 2011, the company moved to Yeoksam-dong, Gangnam-gu. Compared to other stores, relatively small stores are opened.[4]
